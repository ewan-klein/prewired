  1. HTML (semantic & accessible html)

    • Semantic markup is using the right tags for the right things.

    • Accessible means making the content available for people who use websites in different ways (like blind people, people who can't use a mouse, people with really rubbish phones, and other things).

  2. CSS

    • You could use a CSS Reset to normalise everything across different browsers.

    • Try google.com/webfonts for loads of good fonts

    • Do your fonts, colours and stuff first.

    • THEN do layout.

    • Think about responsive web design! (Media queries).
